README file accompanying "The global burden of chronic and hidden hunger revisited: New panel data evidence spanning 1990–2017"

Authors: Bert Lenaerts & Matty Demont

Posted on May, 2022

Suggested reference: Lenaerts, B. 2022. <github.com/BertLenaerts/Lenaerts-and-Demont-2021-Global-Food-Security>

#######################################

VARIABLES:

chronic_hunger, hidden_hunger: chronic and hidden hunger calculated from Godecke, Stein and Qaim (2018) with data from the Global Burden of Diseases, Injuries, and Risk Factors Study 2017 (IHME, 2019)

chronic_hngr_pc,hidden_hngr_pc: chronic and hidden hunger per capita

ISO3: Country 3-letter ISO3 codes

full_name: full name (unofficial)

year

pop: population from UN DESA (2019) with missings filled in from the World Development Indicators (World Bank, 2019) and FOASTAT (2018) data.
